Why Seattle Grill?

Have you ever wanted to hold a barbecue with your friends, but realize you do not have the equipment to hold one or the space to store it? Here at Seattle Grill, we know how cramped city living can be. But that is no excuse not to have fun. What we are doing will change that.

We provide the fun.

At Seattle Grill, we will provide you with all of the equipment to make the rare sunny Seattle day a day to remember!

All day rentals.

Barbecuing with friends and family should always be a pleasant and most importantly, a relaxing affair. That's why we only rent grills by the day. We will deliver them when you want, where you want, and when you are done, we will come pick them up.
